What is QuickCare?

QuickCare is a Company that works with doctors to allow them to aid paitents in the comfort of their own homes at competitive prices.

Important Information:

  • an LLC corporation
  • In the Healthcare industry
  • Focused in aiding the sick and elderly in the comfort of their own home
  • Based in Torrance, California
  • Target Market: sick or elderly individuals who are independent or with families.

How did we think of QuickCare?

Problem: Paitents struggled to move to and from the hospital, constant hospital visits were also expensive

Solution: Create a service that will allow doctors to go to paitent's houses and administer healthcare.

Benefits: Paitents can be in the comfort of their own homes and don't have to pay extremely high hosptial fees
